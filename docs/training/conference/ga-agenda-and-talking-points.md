# GA Conference — Agenda & Talking Points

**Wayfinder suite · Georgia · Employment Specialists only**

**Total time:** 3 hours (180 minutes)  
**Flow:** Walk straight through the [GA ES Conference Binder](./ga-es-conference-binder.md) **Parts A → I**. Open the binder to each part as you teach.

**Pre-conference (separate — not in this block):**

- **Admins & supervisors** — portal, assignments, counselor provisioning, compliance, timesheet approval. Manuals: [Org Admin](../roles/org-admin-manual.md) · [Supervisor](../roles/supervisor-manual.md).
- **Joshua Tree Culture session (earlier)** — values in depth. At **Part H**, point to the binder text; do not re-read all four values aloud unless time allows.

**Handouts:** Printed or PDF binder · [Participant Workbook](./ga-participant-workbook.md) for exercises

## Part A — Quick Reference (5 min)

**Binder page:** URLs, toolkit, support, sidebar layout.

**Talking points:**

- Three apps — Pro, client Wayfinder, Joshua Tree Reports — same mission, different URLs and emails (table on binder).
- Reports replaces **JTSG Reports v2**.
- Sidebar: Daily work / Resources / Account — you will use every item in **Daily work** today.
- **WF-** codes + Bryan for errors.

**Hands-on:** None — orient to binder TOC.

---

## Part B — Getting Signed In (15 min)

**Binder page:** Who uses which app through sign-in troubleshooting.

**Talking points:**

- Walk table: JTSG staff → Pro; participants → Wayfinder; reporting → Reports; counselors → Pro with **agency email**.
- **Google** for `@thejoshuatree.org` on Pro and Reports; Reports = separate session (demo later in Part E).
- **Magic link** — same device; **passkey** optional after first sign-in.
- **Add to Home Screen** — quick demo iPhone + Android.

**Hands-on (Workbook Exercise 0):** Everyone signs in to **Wayfinder Pro**.

---

## Part C — Wayfinder Pro — Daily Casework (40 min)

**Binder page:** Clients through timesheet (Figures 4.1–4.5).

**Talking points:**

- **Clients** — attention sort, badges, open profile.
- **Contact log** — **P0:** counselors see **Notes** only; internal notes stay ES-only; feeds **SE Monthly prefill** (Part E).
- **Applications** — Applied → Interview → Offer → Hired; **job start date** at hire.
- **Messages** — **24 business-hour SLA**; notifications for overdue threads.
- **Timesheet** — log weekly; submit week for supervisor approval.

**Hands-on (Workbook Exercises 1–3, 5):**

- Exercise 1: Log contact (public + internal note).
- Exercise 2: Add/update application.
- Exercise 3: Send test message.
- Exercise 5: One timesheet line.

---

## Part D — Community Partners & Employer Outreach (40 min)

**Binder page:** Map through after the conversation (Figures 5.1–5.2).

**Talking points:**

- Path: Community Partners — map + directory; join link; link employers on client applications.
- **Joshua Tree Mindset** — information gap, not empathy; Golden Rule; acknowledge → pivot.
- Read **opening**, **value prop**, and **ask** from binder (30 sec / 1 min / ask).
- **Four objections** + brush-offs table — hit each in under 60 seconds.
- **After the conversation** — form in CP, log on participant, follow-up.

**Hands-on (Workbook Exercise 4):** Map search + pair role-play opening and one objection.

**Field guide:** [Community Partners Field Guide](../shared/community-partners-field-guide.md) for homework.

---

## Part E — Joshua Tree Reports (35 min)

**Binder page:** Reports intro through troubleshooting (Figures 6.1–6.5).

**Talking points:**

- Reports ≠ Pro casework; official GVRA PDFs → email + Drive.
- Open: Pro → **Reporting** or wayfinder-reports.thejoshuatree.org — sign in separately if needed.
- **Georgia report types** table — master **SE Monthly** first; VPR = copy from contact log detail.
- **Supported Employment Monthly workflow** — six steps on binder.
- **Monthly checklist** + recall/prior month + troubleshooting table.

**Hands-on (Workbook Exercise 6):** GA → SE Monthly → test client → review prefill → sign → submit (if facilitator approves).

## Part F — Onboarding Participants & Counselors (12 min)

**Binder page:** Client setup, counselor setup, employer pointer (Figures 7.1–7.4).

**Talking points:**

- **Client setup** — 10–15 min script; ES checklist on binder; quick-start URL.
- **Counselor setup** — agency email; read-only; coordinate with admin for prerequisites.
- **Employer onboarding** → Part D join link.

**Hands-on (Workbook Exercise 8):** Each ES names one client, one counselor, one employer to onboard this month.
