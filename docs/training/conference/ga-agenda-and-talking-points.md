# GA Training — Agenda & Talking Points

**Wayfinder suite · Georgia · ES + Supervisors + Admins**

Planned as a **full workday** — modules below ~6–7 hours of content; extend with breaks, Q&A, and hands-on practice. **Skip or shorten** modules if you finish early.

---

## Audience

| Track | Who |
|-------|-----|
| **Main** | Employment Specialists, Supervisors |
| **Admin breakout** | Org admins (Module 7) |
| **Materials** | [Participant workbook](./ga-participant-workbook.md), [Slide outline](./ga-slide-deck-outline.md) |

---

## Module 0 — Welcome (30 Min)

**Talking points:**

- Why Wayfinder: one caseload system, client app, formal reports, counselor visibility
- Three apps, one mission — Pro, client Wayfinder, Joshua Tree Reports
- Confidentiality — JTSG staff use `@thejoshuatree.org`; counselors use **GVRA / Tennessee VR agency emails**
- Support: Bryan Evans, WF- error codes

**Activity:** Distribute [Getting signed in](../shared/getting-signed-in.md) — everyone signs in to Wayfinder Pro.

---

## Module 1 — Sign-In & Mobile (45 Min)

**Talking points:**

- Magic link vs passkey — no passwords stored
- Separate Reports sign-in (avoid PKCE confusion)
- Add to Home Screen — demo iPhone + Android

**Hands-on:** All roles sign in; ES opens Clients; admin opens Admin portal.

---

## Module 2 — ES Casework Core (90 Min)

**Talking points:**

- Clients list & attention sort
- Contact log — public notes vs internal notes
- Application pipeline → Hired → job start date
- Messages & 48-hour SLA

**Hands-on (workbook Exercises 1–3):** Log contact, add application, send test message.

**Manual ref:** [ES Manual](../roles/employment-specialist-manual.md)

---

## Module 3 — Community Partners & Employer Outreach (60 Min)

**Talking points:**

- Map + directory
- Public join form for employers
- Script practice in pairs

**Activity:** Role-play [Employer outreach script](../scripts/employer-community-partners-outreach.md) (10 min each).

---

## Module 4 — Timesheet & Exports (45 Min)

**Talking points:**

- ES entry → supervisor approval
- Data exports for grants

**Hands-on:** Enter time; supervisors approve in sandbox or demo week.

---

## Module 5 — Joshua Tree Reports GA (90 Min)

**Talking points:**

- Reporting vs Pro casework
- SE Monthly walkthrough — prefill from contacts
- Other GA forms overview

**Hands-on:** Open Reports from Pro; walk through SE Monthly (test client if available).

**Manuals:** [Reports guide](../reports/joshua-tree-reports-guide.md) + [Quick reference](../reports/joshua-tree-reports-es-quick-reference.md)
