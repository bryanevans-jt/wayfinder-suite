## Part B: Your Role in the System

| You Can | You Typically Do Not |
|---------|----------------------|
| View team caseload signals | Edit another ES’s contact logs |
| Read client message threads (coaching) | Send messages as the ES |
| Approve timesheets | Submit formal reports for ESs (they submit; you review compliance) |
| Run analytics for **your team** | Access super-admin portal settings |
| Coach employer outreach | Replace ES on employer relationships without coordination |

You are the **quality and compliance backstop** for your team — not a second caseload holder.

## Part F: Team Operations & Coaching

### Team Operations

**Path:** Sidebar → **Team operations** (`/dashboard/operations`)

- **Capacity view** — caseload balance across ESs.  
- **Coaching queue** — message SLA overdue, thin contact logs (<4 contacts/month).

Use this page in **weekly 1:1s** with each ES.

> **Screenshot:** `assets/screenshots/supervisor-team-operations.png`

### Coaching Prompts (Use in 1:1s)

- “Show me your Clients list sorted by attention — who’s first?”  
- “Walk me through logging a contact and an application on one profile.”  
- “When is this client’s SE Monthly due?”  
- “Who did you pitch Community Partners to this month?”  
- “Show me a message thread — how fast are you responding?”

### Messages (Read-Only Coaching)

**Path:** Sidebar → **Messages**

View threads to coach response quality and timeliness — **do not** replace the ES in participant conversations.

**Standard:** 48 business-hour response SLA.

## Part I: Community Partners & Employer Outreach

Supervisors coach ESs on employer outreach and may join high-value visits. Full field guide: [Community Partners Field Guide](../shared/community-partners-field-guide.md)

**Path:** Sidebar → **Community Partners**

### The JTSG Mindset

Acknowledge employer concerns → pivot to solutions. **Do not argue.**

### Key Objections (Coach ESs on These)

| Objection | Core Pivot |
|-----------|------------|
| Accommodations cost too much | 50% free; ~$300 average; WOTC |
| No time to train | On-site job coaching included |
| Safety / liability | Safe matching; WEX/OJT option |
| Not hiring | Free pipeline registration — 2 minutes |

### Quick Brush-Offs

- **Too busy** → Leave one-pager; email follow-up.  
- **Corporate** → Get decision-maker name.  
- **Temp agency** → “We complement — free secondary pipeline.”

### After Outreach

Confirm employer in Community Partners; ES logs related contacts.

> **Screenshots:** `es-community-partners-map.png`, `es-community-partners-list.png`
