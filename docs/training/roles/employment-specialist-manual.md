## 3. Clients — Your Home Base

**Path:** Sidebar → **Clients** (`/dashboard/clients`)

- Clients who need follow-up sort toward the **top**.
- **Attention badges** highlight stale contacts or applications.
- Click a client name to open their **profile**.

> **Screenshot:** `assets/screenshots/es-clients-list.png`

### Client Profile Essentials

**Path:** Clients → click client → `/dashboard/clients/[id]`

| Area | What to Do |
|------|------------|
| **Contact log** | Log every call, visit, email — counselors see **Notes** (not internal notes) |
| **Applications** | Track job search pipeline; update status (Applied → Interview → Offer → Hired) |
| **Messages** | Thread with participant (48 business-hour response goal) |
| **Job start date** | Set when hired — triggers 30/60/90-day celebrations for counselors |
| **Formal reports** | History of submitted Joshua Tree Reports for this client |

> **Screenshots:** `es-client-profile-contacts.png`, `es-client-applications.png`

**Suggested contact chips:** Use quick chips when logging common activity types.

### The JTSG Mindset

When an employer hesitates, they usually lack **information**, not empathy. **The Golden Rule:** acknowledge their concern, then pivot to how Supported Employment solves it. Do not argue.

### Top Objections (Summary)

| Objection | Pivot |
|-----------|-------|
| Accommodations cost too much | ~50% free (JAN data); ~$300 average; WOTC |
| No time to train | On-site job coaching — ES trains on-site |
| Safety / liability | Safe job matching; optional WEX/OJT |
| Not hiring now | Free pipeline registration — 2 minutes |

If you do not know the answer: “I will confirm with our team and follow up tomorrow.”
