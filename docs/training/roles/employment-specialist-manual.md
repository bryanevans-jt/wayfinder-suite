## 3. Clients — your home base

**Path:** Sidebar → **Clients** (`/dashboard/clients`)

- Clients who need follow-up sort toward the **top**.
- **Attention badges** highlight stale contacts or applications.
- Click a client name to open their **profile**.

> **Screenshot placeholder — Clients list**  
> File: `assets/screenshots/es-clients-list.png`  
> Show: At least one client row, attention badge if available, search/filter if visible

### Client profile essentials

**Path:** Clients → click client → `/dashboard/clients/[id]`

| Area | What to do |
|------|------------|
| **Contact log** | Log every call, visit, email — counselors see **Notes** (not internal notes) |
| **Applications** | Track job search pipeline; update status (Applied → Interview → Offer → Hired) |
| **Messages** | Thread with participant (48 business-hour response goal) |
| **Job start date** | Set when hired — triggers 30/60/90-day celebrations for counselors |
| **Formal reports** | History of submitted Joshua Tree Reports for this client |

## 11. Helping clients get started

Use the conversation guide: [Client setup guide](../scripts/client-setup-guide-for-es.md)  
Give participants: [Client quick start](../roles/client-quick-start.md) (PDF or in-app link).

**Your checklist:**

1. Confirm participant email in client record.
2. Send Wayfinder URL: `https://wayfinder.thejoshuatree.org`
3. Walk through magic link sign-in (see Getting signed in).
4. Show **Success path**, **Messages**, and optional **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Confirm they can message you and see their milestone path.
