# Wayfinder — Quick start

**For participants · One page**

---

## Sign in

1. Go to **wayfinder.thejoshuatree.org**
2. Enter the **email your Employment Specialist has on file**
3. Tap **Email me a magic link** and open the link in your email
4. Optional: **Sign in with passkey** after your first visit

> **Screenshot:** `assets/screenshots/wayfinder-client-login.png` (captured)

---

## Your dashboard

| Area | What it’s for |
|------|----------------|
| **Success path** | Your program milestones — see where you are |
| **Messages** | Text with your Employment Specialist |
| **Applications** | Jobs you’re pursuing (if shown) |
| **Meetings** | Upcoming appointments |
| **Accessibility** | Larger text / high contrast |

> **Screenshot placeholder — Client dashboard**  
> File: `assets/screenshots/client-dashboard.png`  
> URL: `/dashboard` (signed in as client)

---

## Tips

- **Add to Home Screen** (phone): Share → Add to Home Screen — opens like an app
- **Reply to messages** within a few days — your ES will respond
- **Questions?** Message your ES in the app or call your office

---

## Privacy

Your information is confidential. Do not share your sign-in link with others.
