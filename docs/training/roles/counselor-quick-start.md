# Counselor — Quick start

**Wayfinder Pro · Counselor portal · One page**

---

## Sign in

1. Go to **wayfinder-pro.thejoshuatree.org**
2. Use your **@thejoshuatree.org** email
3. **Email me a magic link** (first time) or **Sign in with passkey**

> **Screenshot:** `assets/screenshots/wayfinder-pro-login.png` (captured — same login as team members)

---

## What you see

Counselors have a **focused view**:

| Menu | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **My clients** | Participants assigned to you — activity timeline |
| **Notifications** | Weekly summaries, hire & 30/60/90-day celebrations |
| **Help / Quick start** | This guide |

You **cannot** edit client records or send messages in Wayfinder Pro.

## Notifications

Open **Notifications** (top of sidebar or mobile menu) for:

- Weekly activity digest
- **Hired** and **30 / 60 / 90 day** employment celebrations

Tap an alert to open that client’s timeline.

---

## If something looks wrong

Contact the participant’s **Employment Specialist** or your program supervisor — do not expect to change data in this portal.
